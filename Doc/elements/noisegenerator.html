Noise Generator

Inputs

MIDI:
This element takes direct input from incoming MIDI signals.

Outputs

AUDIO:
The AUDIO output signal.

Parameters

A:
Attack of the ADSR envelope.

D:
Delay of the ADSR envelope.

S:
Sustain of the ADSR envelope.

R:
Release of the ADSR envelope.
